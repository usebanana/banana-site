What's inside

Small features, sweated over.

🤝

Linked accounts

Pair up with one person. Share tasks, assign work, and see what's been done — all without group-chat overload.

✨

Smart parsing

Type "remind Andi to call the plumber Friday" and Banana fills in the title, assignee, and due date for you.

🔔

Gentle reminders

Set a reminder on any task. Banana nudges you and anyone it's assigned to at the right time.

🌅

My Day

Cut the noise. See only what's yours, today and overdue. Everything else stays out of the way.

📜

Activity history

Know who did what and when. No more "I thought you were doing that."

🏷️

Keywords

Tag tasks with anything that matters to you — groceries, kids, the renovation. Filter to focus.
