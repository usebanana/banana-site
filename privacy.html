Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Banana ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Banana mobile application ("the App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Who We Are

Banana is operated from British Columbia, Canada. You can contact us at: help@usebanana.app

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account information: your name, email address, and username when you register
  • Task data: task titles, notes, checklist items, locations, keywords, reminder times, and any images you attach
  • Profile information: your display name and username

Information Collected Automatically

  • Push notification tokens: a device identifier used to send you push notifications, stored only if you grant permission
  • Activity logs: a record of changes made to tasks (what changed, when, and by which account) for the purpose of showing activity history within the app

Information Processed on Your Behalf

  • AI task parsing: when you use the magic wand feature, the text you have typed into the task title field is sent to Anthropic's Claude API to extract structured information (title, due date, assignees, keywords). This text is processed to provide the feature and is not used to train AI models. See Anthropic's privacy policy at anthropic.com for details on how they handle API data.
  • Location text: locations you type into tasks are stored as text. We do not collect GPS or precise device location.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and operate the App, including syncing tasks between linked accounts
  • Send push notifications about task activity and reminders you have set
  • Display activity history to you and your linked account partners
  • Provide the AI-powered task parsing feature
  • Respond to support requests

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

4. Sharing Your Information

We share your information only in these circumstances:

  • With linked accounts: when you link your account with another user, task data, activity, and your display name and username are shared with that user as part of the core functionality of the App
  • With service providers: we use Supabase (database and storage infrastructure) and Anthropic (AI processing for the task parsing feature). These providers process data on our behalf under their own privacy policies
  • Apple: subscription and payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through In-App Purchase. We do not receive or store your payment information
  • Legal requirements: if required by law or to protect our rights or the safety of our users

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Supabase infrastructure. Data is protected by row-level security policies that ensure users can only access their own data and data explicitly shared with them by linked account partners. We use HTTPS for all data in transit.

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We take reasonable measures to protect your information but cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: you can view your data within the App at any time
  • Correction: you can update your profile and task data within the App
  • Deletion: you can delete your account and all associated data from the Profile screen in the App. This deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
  • Push notifications: you can disable push notifications at any time from your device settings or within the App's profile settings
  • Linked accounts: you can disconnect linked accounts at any time from the Profile screen

If you are a resident of British Columbia or elsewhere in Canada, you may have additional rights under the Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA) or the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA). Contact us at help@usebanana.app to exercise these rights.

7. Subscriptions and Payments

Banana offers a paid subscription processed entirely through Apple In-App Purchase. We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment card information. All billing, refunds, and subscription management are handled by Apple. Please refer to Apple's terms and privacy policy for details.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
